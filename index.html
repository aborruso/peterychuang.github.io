About

Peter Y. Chuang was born in Hong Kong, has once lived in London, and has always wanted to be a Berliner. Once an accomplished pianist, he’s studied psychology and finance, worked in finance, and did financial blogging before turning his attention to to writing novels and short stories.

When he isn't reading or writing, he listens to classical music. He uses Linux (current distro of choice: Arch Linux). Read more about his Linux stuff.

He has a cat.
