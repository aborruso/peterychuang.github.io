About

Peter Y. Chuang was born in Hong Kong. Growing up thinking he would become a concert pianist, he ended up studying Psychology and did some research on language comprehension. That was the time when he realised he would like to become a novelist some day. After spending some time at the London School of Economics, he worked in finance and did some finance blogging before devoting his energy into writing novels.

When he is not reading or writing, he listens to classical music. He uses Linux (current distro of choice: Arch Linux). Read more about his Linux stuff.

He has a cat.
